Our Vision and Mission

Vision: Active representation and participation to ensure good governance and protection of democratic rights and freedom while improving the quality of life of every human being regardless of race, religion, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation or caste.

Mission: To change the political culture of Sri Lanka by establishing ourselves as a political party and driving the change towards a free, just society which upholds and protects individual freedom and rights while entrusting the country's and people's democracy.
