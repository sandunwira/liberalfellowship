Empowering Youth for a Brighter Future

Liberal Fellowship is a youth movement dedicated to creating a just and an equitable society while ensuring individual freedoms and individual rights

Our Work

Our work is focused on creating a just and equitable society while ensuring individual freedoms and rights for all.

Key Focus Areas

Democracy

Promoting active political participation and representation

Liberty

Advocating for individual freedoms and minimizing government intrusion

Civil Rights

Ensuring every individual's rights are protected

Environmental Sustainability

Committing to environmental conservation and addressing climate change

Our Beliefs

We believe in all people's fundamental rights and liberties, and we strive for a lean government that minimizes government intrusion in our everyday lives while maximizing individual and private sector initiatives.

We believe in a democracy, everyone should be able to participate in political life and have a say in the decisions that influence their lives, regardless of their identities, backgrounds, ethnicity, race, religion, gender, sexual orientation and caste.

We believe in equal opportunities for all Sri Lankans; and the encouragement and facilitation of wealth so that everyone can enjoy the greatest possible living, health, education, and social justice standards.

We believe in parliamentary democracy's most basic liberties - are freedom of speech, opinion, worship, expression and association.

We believe in a just and compassionate society and the rule of law and justice are upheld.

We believe that the government should avoid competing with an efficient private sector wherever feasible; and that companies and individuals, not the government, are the actual producers of wealth and jobs.

We believe in conserving the natural beauty and ecosystems of Sri Lanka for future generations and fighting against Global Warming, Climate Change and other contemporary environmental issues.

Our Objectives

To educate the general public of Sri Lanka, mainly the youth about the ideas of democracy, liberty, political and civic rights and their importance.

To provide a platform for the youth of Sri Lanka to debate their views and develop their ideas, in order to develop the society and the country.

To be a voice to the voiceless in terms of injustices and crisis situations.

To empower people to step forward and be leaders in whichever way they can to preserve individual rights and individual freedoms.

To prepare & train young people to participate in and contribute to local government, provincial councils, and legislative processes, with the goal of encouraging them to run for office under our party's banner.
